# Strategy & Planning Prompts

## Strategic Analysis

1. **SWOT Analysis**
   Conduct a SWOT analysis for [company/product/initiative]. Identify 4-5 items in each quadrant with specific, actionable insights rather than generic observations.

2. **PESTLE Analysis**
   Perform a PESTLE analysis for [company/industry]. Cover Political, Economic, Social, Technological, Legal, and Environmental factors affecting the business.

3. **Porter's Five Forces Analysis**
   Analyze [industry/market] using Porter's Five Forces. Assess competitive rivalry, supplier power, buyer power, threat of substitution, and threat of new entry.

4. **Competitive Positioning Analysis**
   Analyze competitive positioning for [company/product] vs [competitors]. Identify our differentiation, gaps, and strategic opportunities.

5. **Value Chain Analysis**
   Map out the value chain for [company/product]. Identify primary and support activities, and highlight areas of competitive advantage or improvement opportunity.

6. **Core Competency Assessment**
   Identify and assess core competencies for [company]. Determine which capabilities provide genuine competitive advantage and which are table stakes.

7. **Market Opportunity Assessment**
   Assess the market opportunity for [product/service/market entry]. Include sizing, growth potential, competitive dynamics, and entry barriers.

8. **Scenario Planning**
   Develop 3 scenarios for [company/industry] over [time horizon]: optimistic, pessimistic, and most likely. Include key drivers and implications of each.

9. **Gap Analysis**
   Conduct a gap analysis comparing current state to desired state for [area]. Quantify gaps where possible and prioritize based on impact.

10. **Stakeholder Analysis**
    Map stakeholders for [initiative/decision]. Identify their interests, influence level, and recommended engagement approach for each.

## Goal Setting & OKRs

11. **Annual Goal Framework**
    Create an annual goal framework for [company/team]. Include 3-5 strategic priorities with measurable outcomes for each.

12. **OKR Development**
    Develop OKRs for [team/company] for [quarter/year]. Create 3 objectives with 3-4 key results each. Ensure key results are measurable and ambitious.

13. **Department Goal Alignment**
    Align [department] goals to company-level OKRs: [paste company OKRs]. Show clear line of sight from team work to company priorities.

14. **KPI Framework**
    Design a KPI framework for [team/function]. Include leading and lagging indicators, targets, measurement frequency, and accountability.

15. **Goal Cascade**
    Cascade this goal to team and individual level: [paste high-level goal]. Maintain alignment while making goals actionable at each level.

16. **Quarterly Planning**
    Structure quarterly planning for [team]. Include priority setting, resource allocation, risk assessment, and success metrics.

17. **Mid-Year Goal Review**
    Create a framework for mid-year goal review. Assess progress, adjust targets if needed, and realign resources for remaining year.

18. **Goal Setting Workshop Design**
    Design a [duration] workshop for [team] to develop their goals. Include activities for brainstorming, prioritization, and commitment.

19. **SMART Goal Conversion**
    Convert these vague goals into SMART goals: [paste goals]. Make them Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound.

20. **Stretch Goal Framework**
    Help set appropriate stretch goals for [area]. Define the difference between committed and aspirational targets.

## Business Planning

21. **Business Plan Outline**
    Create a business plan outline for [venture/initiative]. Include executive summary, market analysis, operations, marketing, financials, and milestones.

22. **Go-to-Market Strategy**
    Develop a go-to-market strategy for [product/service]. Cover target segments, positioning, channels, pricing, and launch timeline.

23. **Growth Strategy**
    Create a growth strategy for [company/product]. Analyze growth levers, prioritize opportunities, and outline execution approach.

24. **Expansion Strategy**
    Develop a strategy for [geographic/market/product] expansion. Include opportunity assessment, entry approach, resource requirements, and risk factors.

25. **Pricing Strategy**
    Develop a pricing strategy for [product/service]. Consider market positioning, competitive analysis, cost structure, and price sensitivity.

26. **Partnership Strategy**
    Create a partnership strategy for [company/initiative]. Identify ideal partner profiles, value propositions, and partnership models to pursue.

27. **Channel Strategy**
    Develop a channel strategy for [product/service]. Analyze channel options, recommend channel mix, and outline channel management approach.

28. **Customer Retention Strategy**
    Create a customer retention strategy for [product/service]. Identify churn drivers, retention levers, and specific initiatives to implement.

29. **Competitive Response Strategy**
    Develop a strategy for responding to [competitive threat]. Include immediate actions, longer-term positioning, and monitoring approach.

30. **Exit Strategy**
    Outline potential exit strategies for [business/investment]. Consider timing, valuation drivers, buyer universe, and preparation steps.

## Operational Planning

31. **Operating Plan Development**
    Create an operating plan for [period] for [team/company]. Include objectives, initiatives, resources, budget, and success metrics.

32. **Resource Allocation Framework**
    Develop a resource allocation framework for [available resources] across [competing priorities]. Include criteria and decision process.

33. **Capacity Planning**
    Create a capacity plan for [function/resource] for [period]. Include demand forecast, current capacity, gap analysis, and scaling plan.

34. **Workflow Optimization**
    Analyze and optimize this workflow: [describe current workflow]. Identify bottlenecks, redundancies, and improvement opportunities.

35. **Process Improvement Plan**
    Create a process improvement plan for [process]. Include current state, target state, improvement actions, and implementation timeline.

36. **Risk Management Plan**
    Develop a risk management plan for [project/initiative]. Identify risks, assess probability and impact, define mitigations, and assign owners.

37. **Contingency Planning**
    Create contingency plans for [scenario/risk]. Include trigger conditions, response actions, responsibilities, and communication plan.

38. **Change Management Plan**
    Develop a change management plan for [change/initiative]. Cover stakeholder analysis, communication, training, and resistance management.

39. **Implementation Roadmap**
    Create an implementation roadmap for [initiative]. Include phases, milestones, dependencies, and success criteria for each phase.

40. **Resource Request Justification**
    Write a justification for requesting [resources] for [initiative]. Include business case, expected outcomes, and alternatives considered.

## Innovation & Growth

41. **Innovation Pipeline**
    Design an innovation pipeline process for [company/team]. Include idea generation, evaluation criteria, testing approach, and scaling framework.

42. **New Product Development Strategy**
    Create a product development strategy for [opportunity area]. Cover market need, product vision, development approach, and success metrics.

43. **Digital Transformation Strategy**
    Develop a digital transformation strategy for [company/function]. Include current state, vision, prioritized initiatives, and implementation approach.

44. **Platform Strategy**
    Design a platform strategy for [product/service]. Consider platform dynamics, ecosystem development, and monetization approach.

45. **Blue Ocean Strategy**
    Apply blue ocean strategy thinking to [product/market]. Identify factors to eliminate, reduce, raise, and create for differentiation.

## Strategic Communication

46. **Strategy Summary**
    Summarize this strategy for [audience]: [paste strategy details]. Tailor level of detail and framing for the specific audience.

47. **Strategy Story**
    Create a narrative that explains our strategy for [initiative]. Make it compelling and memorable for communicating broadly.

48. **Elevator Pitch for Strategy**
    Create a 60-second elevator pitch for [strategy/initiative]. Cover the what, why, and expected outcome concisely.

49. **Strategy FAQ**
    Develop FAQ responses for common questions about [strategy/initiative]. Anticipate concerns and provide clear, aligned answers.

50. **Strategy Update Communication**
    Write an update communication on [strategy] progress. Cover achievements, adjustments, and continued direction for [audience].
