# Business Analysis Prompts

## Market & Industry Analysis

1. **Industry Overview**
   Provide an overview of the [industry] industry. Cover market size, key players, trends, challenges, and outlook.

2. **Market Sizing**
   Help me size the market for [product/service] in [geography/segment]. Include TAM, SAM, SOM with methodology and assumptions.

3. **Market Trend Analysis**
   Analyze trends in [market/industry] that will impact [company/product] over the next [timeframe]. Include implications and recommendations.

4. **Competitor Analysis**
   Analyze [competitor] as a competitor to [our company]. Cover their strategy, strengths, weaknesses, and how we should respond.

5. **Competitive Landscape Mapping**
   Map the competitive landscape for [product/market]. Identify competitors, categorize them, and visualize positioning.

6. **Market Entry Analysis**
   Analyze the opportunity and approach for entering [market/geography]. Cover attractiveness, barriers, and entry strategy options.

7. **Industry Value Chain**
   Map the value chain for [industry]. Identify key players at each stage, margin distribution, and strategic implications.

8. **Substitute Analysis**
   Identify and analyze substitutes for [product/service]. Assess threat level and competitive response options.

9. **Emerging Competitor Assessment**
   Assess [emerging competitor/startup] as a potential threat. Cover their approach, traction, and likelihood of disruption.

10. **Market Forecast**
    Create a [timeframe] forecast for [market]. Include growth projections, key drivers, and scenario variations.

## Financial Analysis

11. **Financial Statement Analysis**
    Analyze these financial statements: [paste or describe]. Identify key insights, trends, and areas of concern or strength.

12. **Ratio Analysis**
    Calculate and interpret key financial ratios for [company/data]. Include profitability, liquidity, efficiency, and leverage ratios.

13. **Variance Analysis**
    Analyze variance between [actual] and [budget/forecast/prior period]. Identify drivers and explain significant differences.

14. **Break-Even Analysis**
    Calculate break-even for [product/business]. Include fixed costs, variable costs, and price to determine units and revenue needed.

15. **Profitability Analysis**
    Analyze profitability by [segment/product/customer]. Identify highest and lowest performers and recommend actions.

16. **Cash Flow Analysis**
    Analyze cash flow for [company/project]. Assess sources and uses, working capital needs, and sustainability.

17. **Investment Analysis**
    Analyze this investment opportunity: [describe]. Calculate NPV, IRR, payback period, and make a recommendation.

18. **Sensitivity Analysis**
    Perform sensitivity analysis on [model/projection]. Identify which variables have greatest impact and risk implications.

19. **Scenario Modeling**
    Create financial scenarios for [situation]. Model best case, worst case, and most likely case with key assumptions.

20. **Valuation Analysis**
    Approach a valuation for [company/asset]. Consider appropriate methodologies and key value drivers.

## Operational Analysis

21. **Process Analysis**
    Analyze this business process: [describe process]. Identify inefficiencies, bottlenecks, and improvement opportunities.

22. **Root Cause Analysis**
    Perform root cause analysis on [problem/issue]. Use 5 Whys or fishbone approach to identify underlying causes.

23. **Capacity Analysis**
    Analyze capacity for [operation/resource]. Assess current utilization, constraints, and scaling options.

24. **Cost Analysis**
    Analyze costs for [product/service/operation]. Break down cost structure, identify drivers, and find reduction opportunities.

25. **Make vs Buy Analysis**
    Analyze whether to make or buy [capability/product]. Include cost comparison, strategic considerations, and recommendation.

26. **Vendor Analysis**
    Analyze [vendor] as a potential supplier. Cover capabilities, pricing, reliability, and strategic fit.

27. **Productivity Analysis**
    Analyze productivity for [team/operation]. Define appropriate metrics, assess current performance, and identify improvement levers.

28. **Quality Analysis**
    Analyze quality performance for [product/service]. Review defect rates, customer complaints, and improvement priorities.

29. **Supply Chain Analysis**
    Analyze supply chain for [product/operation]. Map the chain, identify risks, and recommend optimization.

30. **Benchmarking Analysis**
    Benchmark [our performance] against [comparators]. Identify gaps, best practices to adopt, and realistic targets.

## Customer & Business Analysis

31. **Customer Segmentation**
    Segment customers for [business/product]. Identify meaningful segments, their characteristics, and size/value of each.

32. **Customer Profitability Analysis**
    Analyze customer profitability for [customer segment/group]. Identify most and least profitable, and recommend actions.

33. **Churn Analysis**
    Analyze customer churn for [product/service]. Identify patterns, likely causes, and retention opportunities.

34. **Customer Lifetime Value**
    Calculate and analyze CLV for [customer segment]. Include methodology and implications for acquisition spending.

35. **Win/Loss Analysis**
    Analyze win/loss data: [paste or describe data]. Identify patterns in why we win or lose deals.

36. **Sales Analysis**
    Analyze sales performance: [paste or describe data]. Identify trends, top performers, and areas needing attention.

37. **Pricing Analysis**
    Analyze pricing for [product/service]. Consider cost, value, competition, and price elasticity.

38. **Product Portfolio Analysis**
    Analyze the product portfolio using BCG matrix or similar. Categorize products and recommend portfolio actions.

39. **Channel Analysis**
    Analyze [sales/distribution] channel performance. Assess effectiveness, costs, and optimization opportunities.

40. **Business Model Analysis**
    Analyze the business model for [company]. Map using business model canvas and identify strengths and vulnerabilities.

## Strategic Analysis

41. **Strategic Options Analysis**
    Analyze strategic options for [decision/situation]. Identify alternatives, evaluate pros/cons, and recommend path.

42. **Acquisition Target Analysis**
    Analyze [company] as potential acquisition target. Cover strategic fit, valuation considerations, and integration complexity.

43. **Partnership Opportunity Analysis**
    Analyze partnership opportunity with [company]. Assess strategic value, compatibility, and deal structure options.

44. **Portfolio Analysis**
    Analyze [business/investment] portfolio. Assess performance, risk, diversification, and rebalancing recommendations.

45. **Synergy Analysis**
    Analyze potential synergies from [merger/acquisition/partnership]. Quantify revenue and cost synergies realistically.

46. **Risk Analysis**
    Analyze risks for [business/project/decision]. Identify, assess probability and impact, and prioritize.

47. **Opportunity Cost Analysis**
    Analyze opportunity costs of [decision/direction]. What are we giving up by choosing this path?

48. **Exit Analysis**
    Analyze exit options for [business/investment]. Consider timing, valuation, and exit routes.

49. **Strategic Fit Analysis**
    Analyze strategic fit of [opportunity] with [company strategy]. Assess alignment and trade-offs.

50. **Impact Analysis**
    Analyze the impact of [change/decision/event] on [business/operations]. Consider direct and indirect effects.
